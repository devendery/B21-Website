    Polygonscan Verification — Description (Copy)

    Block21 (B21) is a fixed-supply ERC-20 token deployed on Polygon with immutable tokenomics and no administrative control after launch.

The contract mints exactly 2,100,000 B21 (8 decimals) once during deployment. No further minting, burning, or supply modification is possible.

A fixed 2% fee is applied only when B21 is sold to the official DEX liquidity pair. Wallet-to-wallet transfers, DEX buys, and liquidity operations are fee-free. The sell fee is split automatically and sent directly to predefined Treasury and Rewards wallets.

The liquidity pair address is set once after deployment to resolve DEX pool creation dependency, after which ownership is permanently renounced. This prevents any future changes to fees, liquidity routing, or token behavior.

The contract contains no upgrade mechanisms, no pausing logic, no blacklists, and no external calls in the fee path. After ownership renunciation, Block21 operates as a fully decentralized and immutable ERC-20 token.

Any future economic logic (V2) is designed to operate externally and cannot modify this contract.
